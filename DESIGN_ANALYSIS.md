# Soho Lighting Design Analysis

## Visual Foundation
- **Primary Background**: Warm beige/cream (#F7F7F0)
- **Text Color**: Dark slate gray (#373A36)
- **Accent Colors**: Muted greens and warm metallics (aged brass, bronze)
- **Typography**: Proxima Nova (sans-serif) for body, Baskerville Display (serif) for display text
- **Hero Background**: Soft, blurred image with warm tones (lighting fixtures in interior)

## Key Sections Identified
1. **Header**: Sticky navigation with logo, menu, search, account, cart
2. **Hero Section**: Full-width background image with centered text overlay ("It's in the detail")
3. **Features Section**: Three columns with icons (Environmentally Conscious, Considered Design, Pioneering Innovation)
4. **Popular Categories**: Carousel with product category cards
5. **Sockets & Switches Section**: Featured product showcase with background texture
6. **Collections Grid**: Multiple product collections displayed in a grid
7. **Testimonial Section**: Customer quote with attribution
8. **Outdoor Lighting Section**: Split layout with image and text
9. **Trending Products**: Carousel with product cards showing prices
10. **Footer**: Links and additional information

## Design Philosophy
- **Luxury & Craftsmanship**: Emphasis on quality, handcrafted details
- **Warm, Organic Aesthetic**: Natural materials, muted color palette
- **Elegant Typography**: Mix of serif and sans-serif for hierarchy
- **Spacious Layout**: Generous whitespace and breathing room
- **Subtle Interactions**: Smooth transitions and hover effects

## Color Palette
- Background: #F7F7F0 (warm cream)
- Text: #373A36 (dark slate)
- Accent Green: #6B8E7F (muted sage)
- Accent Gold/Brass: #C9A961 (warm metallic)
- Borders: Light gray (#E8E8E0)

## Typography System
- Display: Baskerville Display PT (serif), 80px, font-weight 400
- Heading: Proxima Nova (sans-serif), various sizes
- Body: Proxima Nova (sans-serif), 16px, font-weight 400
- Letter Spacing: Generous (0.16px to 0.35px)
